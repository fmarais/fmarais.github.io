Delete your account
Petrol & Diesel Price Tracker

Instructions for permanently deleting your account and personal data from the Petrol & Diesel Price Tracker app (com.xenicode.petrol_diesel_price_tracker), published by xeni CODE.

Last updated: 14 May 2026 Publisher: xeni CODE App: Petrol & Diesel Price Tracker

1. How to delete your account in the app

Account deletion is available directly inside the app. No support request is required. Follow these steps:

  1. 01 Open the app and tap the Profile tab (bottom-right icon).
  2. 02 Scroll to the bottom of the screen and tap Delete account (shown in red).
  3. 03 Read the confirmation message and enter your password to verify your identity.
  4. 04 Tap Delete my account. The app will delete your profile data and sign you out immediately.

The deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

2. What data is deleted

The table below describes what happens to each category of data when you delete your account.

Data Action Notes
Firebase Authentication account (email, password hash) Deleted Removed from Firebase Auth immediately.
User profile & points balance Deleted The users/{uid} document is removed.
Points ledger (transaction history) Deleted All entries under users/{uid}/pointsLedger are removed.
Vote mirror (which prices you voted on) Deleted All entries under users/{uid}/votes are removed.
Stations you created Kept (anonymised) Stations are crowd-sourced community data. They remain visible to other users. The createdBy field (your uid) is retained in the document but is no longer linked to any account.
Price entries you submitted Kept (anonymised) Price entries are crowd-sourced community data and remain in the database. The capturedBy uid is retained but is no longer linked to any account.
Votes you cast on other prices Kept (score impact only) The aggregated score on each price entry reflects your votes but individual vote documents will be cleaned up in a future background process.

3. Retention period

Personal account data (authentication credentials, profile, points ledger, vote mirror) is deleted immediately when you confirm deletion in the app. There is no waiting period and no soft-delete — the data cannot be recovered after you confirm.

Crowd-sourced station and price data is retained indefinitely as community data and is not considered personal data under our privacy policy.

If you no longer have access to the app or your account and need to request deletion by email, contact us at the address below. Include the email address associated with your account so we can locate and remove it.
